Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly — such as your name, email address, and password when you create an account. We also collect the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not access your bank accounts or financial institutions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. We use your expense entries to display summaries, patterns, and insights within your account. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal financial data with advertisers or third-party marketers.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers with appropriate encryption and access controls. We retain your account and expense data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your data at any time, and upon account deletion, we will remove your information within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties except as required to operate the service — for example, hosting infrastructure providers. We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across other websites.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond to your inquiry within a reasonable timeframe.
